About Know IT HUB

Know IT HUB is a comprehensive knowledge management platform specifically designed for the Systems & IT Department at Dubai Duty Free. Our platform serves as the central repository for technical documentation, code snippets, procedures, and team resources.

Built to streamline information sharing and improve operational efficiency, Know IT HUB empowers our IT professionals with instant access to critical knowledge and resources.
